Commercial & Industrial Epoxy Flooring

Commercial and industrial floors take a beating — forklifts, foot traffic, chemicals, and constant cleaning. Professional epoxy and polyaspartic systems stand up to it while keeping the space bright, safe, and easy to maintain.

Common Applications

  • Warehouses and distribution centers
  • Auto shops, dealerships, and service bays
  • Retail showrooms and restaurants
  • Manufacturing, commercial kitchens, and hangars

Built for Uptime

Fast-cure polyaspartic systems let many commercial floors go back into service quickly — often within a day — minimizing downtime. Installers can add slip-resistant textures and chemical-resistant topcoats matched to how the space is used.
