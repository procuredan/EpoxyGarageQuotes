Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 14, 2026

Epoxy Garage Quotes ("we," "us," or "our") operates epoxygaragequotes.com. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use our website and request a review or quote.

Information We Collect

When you submit a request, we collect the information you provide, such as your name, email address, phone number, organization or association name, and details about the services you are interested in. We also automatically collect limited technical data such as your IP address, browser type, referring page, and advertising click identifiers (for example, Google Click ID and UTM parameters) to measure and improve our marketing.

How We Use Your Information

  • To contact you about your request and the services you asked us to review
  • To prepare and deliver competitive quotes, reviews, and related recommendations
  • To operate, measure, and improve our website and advertising
  • To comply with legal obligations

How We Share Your Information

We share your information with Procure Media, LLC and licensed specialists or service providers who help fulfill your request. We do not sell your personal information. We may disclose information when required by law.

Separately from text messaging: when you request a quote, the contact information you provide (name, phone number, and email address) is shared with the local garage floor coating installers in our network so that they can contact you by phone and email with quotes for your project. This sharing applies to phone calls and email only — it does not apply to text messaging, and installers are never given your SMS consent.

Communications Consent

By submitting a request, you consent to be contacted by Epoxy Garage Quotes and its partners by phone, text message, and email, including through automated technology, regarding your request. Consent is not a condition of purchase. You may opt out of marketing communications at any time by replying STOP to texts, clicking unsubscribe in emails, or contacting us at quotes@epoxygaragequotes.com.

SMS / Text Messaging

Epoxy Garage Quotes (Procure Media, LLC) sends text messages only to people who request a quote on epoxygaragequotes.com and choose to opt in, and only to follow up on that request (for example, a confirmation and status updates about your quote). We do not send marketing or promotional texts. Your mobile phone number and your SMS consent are never sold, shared, or transferred to any third party, and no third party sends you text messages using this consent.

Message frequency varies. Message and data rates may apply. Reply STOP to opt out at any time, or HELP for help.

Cookies & Analytics

We use cookies and analytics tools (including Google Analytics and Google Ads) to understand site usage and measure advertising performance. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

Your Choices & Rights

Depending on your state, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete your personal information, or to opt out of certain processing. To exercise these rights, contact us at quotes@epoxygaragequotes.com.
